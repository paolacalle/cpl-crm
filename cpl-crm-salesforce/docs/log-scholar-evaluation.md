# How to Log an Evaluation for a Call (Scholar Lead)

A quick guide for logging an evaluation — for example, after a phone call with a
scholar lead. Each interview, call, or interaction can have its own evaluation,
and the lead's **Average Evaluation Rating** updates automatically.

## Steps

1. **Open the scholar lead.**
   Go to the **Leads** tab (or the CPL app) and open the scholar lead you spoke with.

2. **Find the Actions & Recommendations panel.**
   On the lead record, look for the **Actions & Recommendations** panel (the list of
   suggested actions, usually on the side of the page).

3. **Click "Lead Scholar Evaluation."**
   It is pinned to the **top** of the list. This opens the guided evaluation form.

4. **Screen 1 — Interaction & Rating.**
   - **Evaluation Type:** choose **Call** (use *Interview*, *Mentorship*, or *Other*
     for different interactions).
   - **Evaluation Date:** defaults to today — change it if the call was earlier.
   - **Rating:** pick **1–5** for how the interaction went. *(Required — the lead's
     average rating is built from this.)*
   - **Strengths / Areas for Improvement / Additional Comments:** fill in any notes.
   - Click **Next**.

5. **Screen 2 — Evaluator.**
   - **Evaluator Type:** choose **User** (a CPL team member, like you) or **Contact**
     (an external person, e.g., a coach).
   - **Evaluator Relationship** and **Feedback Source:** select if relevant
     (Feedback Source = **Call** here).
   - Then pick the evaluator: the **Evaluator User** box (for User) or
     **Evaluator Contact** box (for Contact) appears based on your choice.
   - Click **Next**.

6. **Finish.**
   You'll see "Evaluation saved." Click **Finish**.

## Where it shows up

- The new evaluation appears in the **Evaluations** related list on the lead.
- The lead's **Average Evaluation Rating** and **Evaluation Count** update automatically.
- When the lead is later converted, all evaluations move onto the CPL Member account.

## Optional: attach the actual call to the evaluation

If you want the phone call itself in the activity timeline, open the new
evaluation record and use the **Activity** panel → **Log a Call**. The call is
saved with **Related To = this Evaluation**.

> Note: a call logged directly on the lead can't also be attached to the
> evaluation (Salesforce limitation), so log it on the **evaluation** record.
