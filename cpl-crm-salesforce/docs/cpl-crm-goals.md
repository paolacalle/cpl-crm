# CPL CRM Goals

The CPL CRM tracks:
- CPL Members
- Prospect Applications
- Employment
- Evaluations
- Intake users
- Scholarship finalist workflows
- Resume and recommendation document collection
- Admin visibility and review processes

Important requirements:
- Admin users should be able to view and manage CPL records.
- Evaluations may relate to Prospect Applications, Employment, or CPL-only feedback.
- Some evaluators are internal users; others are external contacts.
- Flows should be easy for non-technical users to follow.
